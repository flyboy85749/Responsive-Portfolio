About Me

I've had many roles over the years, from son and brother, to pilot and professional photographer, but the role that resonates most with me today is that of web developer.

I was born and raised in Pennsylvania, and moved to Arizona in 2000, with my then wife, four dogs and a parakeet. Since then I have had many avocations, but have always been searching for that vocation that both challenges me and holds my interest. I love learning, coding, and solving problems; web development seemed to be the perfect fit.

So, I got some library books on HTML/CSS and PHP/MySql, and taught myself enough to get some friends and family to allow me to build websites for them. From there, I eventually built up a part-time freelance business. That was about ten years ago or so, and now I am starting over in a new town, with new skills, and new clients.

If you are looking for someone to create a website or to build an application that is uniquely yours, be sure to contact me for a quote or to address any questions or concerns you may have before we begin.
